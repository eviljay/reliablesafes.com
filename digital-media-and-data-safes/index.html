In an information age, data is a critical asset. The susceptibility to climate fluctuations, humidity, cold, heat, water or fire, can destroy years of valued and irreplaceable information. Data safes are designed to secure the valued assets that your or your business rely on. Data safes are designed and constructed to protect disks and other audio and video storage devices such as video tapes and SD cards from fire and climate fluctuations.

Most digital media is more fragile and susceptible to damage than paper documents and as such requires additional protection. A data safe must keep the interior temperature below 135 degrees and the humidity level below 85 percent. Valuable data must be protected and secured from fire, theft or climate changes.

Data or digital media safes are a must for any business or home office that stores important information in a digital format. The combination of the security of a fire safe with the interior protection for data makes our safes a wise choice for any function.

Did you know eight businesses are damaged by fire every hour in the United States? Unfortunately, the majority of businesses were not prepared for the protection of their critical records. When vital information is destroyed businesses can have a very difficult time recovering. From the largest of companies to home office every one needs fire and environmental protection for their digital media. The security and protection of such vital information is essential for the continuance of any business.
