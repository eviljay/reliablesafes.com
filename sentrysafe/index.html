What SentrySafe Brings To You

Lifetime after-fire replacement guarantee – your SentrySafe product will be replaced if destroyed in a fire and shipping will be free! SentrySafe also offers up to $50,000 fire protection limited reimbursement guarantee on our steel Fire-Safe® products – which reimburses consumers for direct physical loss of contents caused by a fire.

SentrySafe started manufacturing safes in 1932 and since their very first model they have continued to make impressive and ground-breaking improvements, such as the first UL classified fire-resistant safe in 1955, setting new standards of fire resistance, improving locking technology and launching the worlds first fire-resistant data security product with USB access.
