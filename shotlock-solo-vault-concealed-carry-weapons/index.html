Shotlock Solo-Vault CCW

  
 

The Shotlock Solo-Vault Concealed Carry Weapons CCW safe is the best in compact pistol security on the market today.  You combine a sleak design with 14 gauge steel construction and you get a safe that can be stored anywhere, mounted anywhere but still gives you the security you need.


  

 

With a quality product made right here in the USA you can be sure that you will love your Shotlock solo-vault CCW.

The Shot Lock Solo Vault CCW is made to hold all compact and subcompact pistols up to a .380 and 9mm.  The interior dimensions are 7"x5"x2".  It can be mounted virtually anywhere and programmed with up to 1000 different combinations.
